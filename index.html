Comments, Questions, and Concerns

The team discussed the importance of being proactive and not waiting until the last minute to complete major deliverables.

It was noted that the team did put in effort for the experiment itself, but the final deliverables were weaker than expected because important required items were missing and the report lacked enough clarity.

The team also talked about how asking for help earlier would have made the process easier instead of waiting until the deadline got too close.

Unfinished Business

The team still needs to fully decide how responsibilities will be divided for future assignments, especially for slides, video preparation, formatting, and final submission checks.

New Business

New ideas brought up during the meeting:

  • Create a checklist for every assignment deliverable before submission
  • Prepare slide decks earlier so the final video has better structure and context
  • Ask the TA questions sooner when assignment instructions are unclear
  • Communicate sooner if deadlines seem difficult to meet
  • Make sure every team member contributes proactively instead of waiting for direction
